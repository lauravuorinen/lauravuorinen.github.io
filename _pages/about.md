I am a space physicist working as a postdoctoral researcher at Queen Mary University of London. My main research interests include the solar wind-magnetosphere interaction, collisionless shocks, particle acceleration, and plasma turbulence. My current research focuses on the interaction of plasma turbulence with collisionless shock waves. My work utilizes results from computer simulations and spacecraft observations.
